Who We Are

Where Science Meets the Universe

Techfest is the annual science and technology festival of IIT Bombay — Asia's largest of its kind. Born in 1998, it has grown from a campus event into a global celebration of human ingenuity, drawing participants, innovators, and Nobel laureates from over 60 countries.

From robotics battles to astrophysics lectures, from AI hackathons to art installations powered by code — Techfest is where the boundaries between disciplines dissolve.

Legacy

A Journey Through Time

1998
The First Spark
A small group of IIT Bombay students organise the inaugural Techfest, a weekend of talks and competitions that ignites a tradition.
2004
Going International
Foreign universities send their first delegations. International participation doubles year-on-year and a new era begins.
2012
Asia's Largest
Officially recognised as Asia's largest science & technology festival. Over 100,000 participants. A Guinness-worthy milestone.
Today
A Global Platform
190+ countries, Nobel laureates on stage, ₹1Cr+ prize pool, and a community of innovators shaping the future — the journey continues.
